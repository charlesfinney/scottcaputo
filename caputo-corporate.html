Corporate & Commercial Video And Crane cinematography Production.

Scott Caputo Ltd. is a Los Angeles-based corporate video and Crane cinematography production company specialising in creative video content for a digital audience. With our origins in traditional and Crane cinematography, we have grown the business to be able to offer a wide range of video production capabilities throughout America,Europe and worldwide including: licensed and legal drone aerial video, 2D & 3D Crane cinematography, cinematic film production and much more to clients ranging from television, and businesses to bands. Driven to succeed, we see each project as an opportunity to not only exceed our clients expectations but also to take our capabilities as a film & video production company to the next level in a world where rapidly changing technology presents us with new opportunities on a daily basis. Scott Caputo Ltd. prides itself not only on the quality of the work, but also the importance of a smooth client experience from idea generation through to delivery of the final product. Different clients have different needs in this respect and we are sure to tailor our service accordingly.

Licensed, Legal Cinematic Drone Aerial Video

With the tremendous advances in drone technology (aerial, quadcopter or UAV as we prefer to call it), Scott Caputo has been at the leading edge of the new wave of drone cinematographers for the last few years. Working in conjunction with our crane and Jib cinematography, we bring our aerial video expertise not only to full-service projects that we manage for our clients but also on a 3rd party basis for other production companies that require our aerial video capabilities. Used correctly, aerial filming helps tell a story like no other. Be it jaw-dropping ‘establishing’ shots at the start of a film or close-quarters, technical moves that replicate a Hollywood-style jib or crane, Scott Caputo has the skills to provide what our clients require. We are filmmakers first and Crane and drone pilots second. Watch our showreel and you will see the difference this makes. Scott Caputo is fully insured, Crane qualified with up-to-date permissions for commercial crane and aerial work and adheres to the strict rules and regulations set by the American Society of Cinematographers and Civil Aviation Authority.
